Residual-Demand Allocation Impact (Australia Rooftop)
===================================================

Interpretation
--------------
The new run allocates utility generation to residual demand after household rooftop
generation is deducted. This reduces denominator inflation in total system capacity.

Conclusion
----------
If the decline becomes visibly less steep in the new run, that supports the hypothesis
that the previous drop was dominated by system denominator growth rather than only
household rooftop saturation.
